The sound of the Indian Matka sampled from a Matka, the circumference of which is 30 inches. The sound could be incorporated into a musical piece to add an Indian flavor. The humble Matka, a traditional Indian clay pot, can be a surprisingly versatile addition to your music. While its size in this case (30-inch circumference) might influence the pitch slightly, the sampled sound itself holds a wealth of sonic possibilities. Its characteristic rattle can be layered with percussion to create a driving, earthy groove.
